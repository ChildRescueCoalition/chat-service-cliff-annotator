VIDEO: Indonesia's super rich help the poor Indonesia has one of Asia's fastest growing population of high net worth individuals, yet more than half of Indonesians still live on under two dollars per day. With growing evidence that the wealth gap may widen even further, many of Indonesia's rich have set up charitable foundations to help the poor in areas like education and healthcare. The BBC's Karishma Vaswani reports on one Indonesian billionaire's plans to help his fellow citizens.
